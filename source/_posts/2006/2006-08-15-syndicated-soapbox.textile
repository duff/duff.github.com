The geeks out there using "Soapbox":http://soapboxit.com will be excited to get their hands on some XML feeds.  You can subscribe to the following:
* The reviews in your network
* The recent reviews
* The reviews of an individual person.

If you don't know what syndication feeds are, you can learn about them "here":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atom_%28standard%29.
